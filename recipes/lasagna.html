Lasagna



Prep: 1hr | Cook: 2hrs | Additional: 10 mins | Total: 3 hrs 10 mins
Servings: 12 | Yield: 1 9x13-inch pan

Vegetable lasagna with all the trimmings. This recipe doesn't miss a beat!

Ingredients

    Sauce:
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano

Directions

  1. heat everything up
  2. mix everything together
  3. preheat oven
  4. bring water to boil, add noodles
  5. put everything together and bake it
